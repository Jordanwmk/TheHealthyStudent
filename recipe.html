Chicken Stir Fry

Ready in: 30 min

Serves: 4

Weekly Menu

Ingredients

For the Stir Fry

2 tablespoons olive oil

3 cloves garlic, minced

200g button mushrooms, sliced thinly

For the Marinade

1 tablespoon lemon juice

1/2 tablespoon honey

1 tablespoon sesame oil

Method

1. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, lemon juice, honey, sesame oil and rice wine vinegar. Add in the chicken, and toss to coat. Let marinate in the fridge will you chop your veggies.

2. When you have all your veggies ready to go, heat a large wok over high heat. Add one table-spoon of the olive oil.

3. Drain the chicken from the marinade, reserving it. Add the chicken to the wok and cook until the chicken is cooked through, about five minutes. Remove the chicken to a plate.

4. Add the remaining olive oil to the wok, and then add in the garlic. Cook for a minute or until fragrant. Add in the carrots, bell peppers, snow peas, and mushrooms. Cook until bright in colour and just crisp cooked, about three minutes.

5. Add in the broccoli and cooked chicken. Reduce heat to low. Bring the wok to a simmer and cook until thick, about five minutes. Serve on top of Minute Brown Rice and topped with green onions and toasted sesame seeds.
